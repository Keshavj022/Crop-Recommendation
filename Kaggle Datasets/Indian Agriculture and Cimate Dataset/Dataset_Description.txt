About Dataset
Indian Agriculture and Climate Dataset (1961-2018)

Overview:

This dataset provides a comprehensive collection of agricultural and climate data for India spanning the years 1961 to 2018. It covers various crops, rainfall, and temperature statistics, offering valuable insights into the dynamics of agricultural production and climate patterns in the region.

Key Insights:

Analyze crop-specific trends, production estimates, and area harvested over the study period.
Explore the interplay between rainfall patterns and crop yields.
Investigate the impact of temperature variations on agricultural productivity.
Identify potential correlations between climatic factors and specific crop performance.
Potential Use Cases:

Climate change impact assessments on agriculture.
Crop-specific research and development initiatives.
Acknowledgments:

Source: Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO).
Rainfall and temperature data sourced from reliable meteorological databases.
